amaysim Mates

Facebook Competition

Amaysim mates was a social media (facebook) campaign to get people thinking how they them self rate as a mate and then push it onto their friends to let them know how much they rock.

Of course, no comp would be complete with out an incentive of a good prize.

Friendship these days is often determined by how many likes to a post or how many photos make it to a timeline. So I chose to base the concept around imagery, selecting images that capture different mateships.
