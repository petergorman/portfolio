Launch Landing Page

Goodtrip is a startup travel company that offers their clients only the best travel options from more detailed profiling.

To generate interest and move towards a site launch, I created a single page landing page that captures peoples email address to offer promotions and details on the features of the site and launch date.

The client requested full screen video and a responsive experience. I opted for HTML5 fullscreen video and targeted specific browsers through conditional code.

With the responsive element, I opted to not attempt the video so not to suck dry the potential limited bandwidth and 3G connection, meaning the page was quick to load for the user.
