Rosemount

Desktop Application and Website

Rosemount Diamond in You was a campaign that ran in-line with Australian Fashion Week. Rosemount captured the full event and delivered it through this neat desktop application, displaying photos, videos, commentary and tweets.

The design of the app captured the spirit of fashion week and Rosemount's involvement with simple shapes allowing the focus to be content and brand. Navigation was kept simple with tabs and pagination.

The app was also later turned into a website post event to keep the content alive.
