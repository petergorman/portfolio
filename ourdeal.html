Ourdeal

Website and iPhone App

Ourdeal is one of Australia's leading group buying websites offering discounted pricing on experiences and services.

Being heavily involved in the startup of this business I developed the brand and logo to create a trusted symbol that gives the user confidence in their purchase.

Content is king for this site, so when it came to UI design it was all about displaying the content with minimal clutter and create white space where possible to push eyes to action.
