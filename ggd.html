Green Golf Deals

Brand Development

Green Golf Deals is a new daily deals website exculsively for golf nuts. Run by PGA Professional Greg Green, Green Golf Deals will offer it's members discounted golf experiences from half price green fees at Australia's top courses to discounted top line equipment.

For the brand I aimed to present GGD as a premium company with a sports edge, along the lines of Taylormade and Nike. In a business offering discounted goods it's important to remain premium.

The landing page offers a tease of whats to come but also a glimpse at the stylish premium feel the full site will offer.
