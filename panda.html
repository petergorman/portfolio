PandaPaks

WebSite

PandaPaks is a subscription based website offering time poor parents an online way to have nappies delivered to thier door. My involvement was to develop the brand and logo then carry the style through to pre launch landing pages and the full site.

I collaborated with talented backend developers to build the full front end of the site utilising bootstrap framework.
